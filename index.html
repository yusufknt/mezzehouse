About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Chef Michael Laurent, Savoria was born from a passion for creating exceptional dining experiences that celebrate the finest ingredients and culinary traditions from around the world.

What began as a small, intimate dining room has evolved into one of the city's most beloved culinary destinations, known for its commitment to excellence, innovation, and warm hospitality.

Our Philosophy

At Savoria, we believe that great food begins with great ingredients. We source locally whenever possible, working directly with farmers, fishermen, and artisanal producers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Each dish on our menu is thoughtfully crafted to showcase the natural flavors of these exceptional ingredients, complemented by house-made components and precise technique.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Michael Laurent

With over 20 years of experience in renowned kitchens across Europe and America, Chef Michael brings a wealth of knowledge, creativity, and passion to Savoria's kitchen. His innovative approach to classic techniques has earned the restaurant numerous accolades and a loyal following.
